How to use Walk a Pal!

Sign up & Sign in

Sign up by typing in your email and password and press the "Sign Up" button. If you forgot your password, you can click the reset password button, or if you don't an account, you can click sign up! Just follow the instructions on the screen then click enter. After, you will receive an email asking you to verify your email by pressing the link sent in the email.

Choose Pet Screen

After creating an accouunt, you are brought to a screen asking you to choose from 1 of 4 dogs. Once you've picked your favourite dog, press confirm and continue to the app.

Workout Screen

To start your first work out press the "Click to start workout" button on the profile screen. Here you will be prompted with a start workout button, and end workout button, and a back button that takes you back to the home screen.

Water Screen

This screen can be accesses by clicking the "Water intake" button on the main screen. here you can click the "+" button everytime you drinkl a cup of water. Once you've drank 5 cups of water you will get 5 coins.

Shop

The shop screen can be accessed by clicking the "shop" button in the top left corner of the profile screen. Once there you can use the tabs at the top to toggle between your shopping options. To buy an item you just have to click it and press "buy".

Log-Out

To logout of the app all you need to do is click the "logout" button on the bottom right of the profile screen



Notice we are equally compatible with phones and tablets. You'll see on the Homepage, and "About us!" page that the images featured of the app are running on a tablet. Meanwhile, the gifs above are on a phone. The usability and the appearance should not change no matter what device you are using.
